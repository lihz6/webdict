hello webdict

Hi, I happen to have a Chrome Extension called `webdict` you can use to help things along when you enccounter new words as you browse the web. Viewing definitions and playing sounds can come in handy. It also manages a history of words you've looked up in case you feel like practising them later on. It is still evolving though and we are commited to making it a little better day by day. But more importantly, it requires you to involve to make it even better.
